Welcome to PhysiQuest!

PhysiQuest is an application designed to help you manage quests and improve your skills in a fun and engaging way. Our platform allows you to search, complete, and assign quests to other users, all while tracking your progress and earning rewards.

How it works

  1. Search Quests: Use the search feature to find quests by title, description, reward points, or tokens. You can also view quests assigned to specific users by entering their username.
  2. Complete Quests: Once you find a quest you'd like to complete, click the "Complete" button and provide your username and an image URL as proof of completion. You'll earn reward points and tokens upon successful completion of a quest.
  3. Assign Quests to Users: If you'd like to assign a quest to another user, click the "Assign" button and enter their username. They will be notified of the new quest and can complete it to earn rewards.

Ready to embark on your questing journey? Start searching for quests now, or create an account to track your progress and unlock additional features!
